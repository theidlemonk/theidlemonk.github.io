Our Vision

Rehabilitating American Pride

Genesis Communities believes the most important investment, is the investment in life An investment that provides a community with safe affordable housing and events that teach positive lifestyle choices with uncompromising commitments that benefits the residents and society.

About

Genesis Community | OHIO Non-Profit Corp

  • The Dream

    Most of us dream of having a small place on a lake or in the woods, somewhere out in the country, a safe place for privacy and solitude, a place to call home.

  • Our Veterans

    For a segment of those individuals, ones who have paid their debts to society, including a number of proud American Veterans that have been pre-screened by the Department of Corrections, individuals they feel are ready to integrate back into society, a safe place to live and work or a community that would accept them is even harder for them to realize.

  • Our Country

    Facts show in this great country, there are thousands of homeless Veterans who need a place to home who have not been incarcerated, and yet, live daily within an invisible prison, one without walls and iron bars, a prison called despair and hopelessness!

  • Genesis Community

    Genesis Communities (An Ohio Based Not For Profit Corporation) has a solution. Our designs of personal cabins, ones that offer a comfortable and ecological way of living in harmony with local and State guidelines, sensitive to the NIMBY laws. (Not In My Backyard) gives these individuals a sense of hope. A place where they feel a sense of community involvement and have affordable housing, a community that offers jobs and support by surrounding them with positive thinking, social, faith based and professional individuals that promotes positive lifestyles to enable the residents to become welcomed contributors back to society.
