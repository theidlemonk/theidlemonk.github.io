Conflict

Sunday, 15 February 2015

Back to Blog Home

Let me tell you about a scene from my life that challenged me in multiple forms. It was one of them simple 'lets go out to eat' moments. But this had been building up from before. We would never go to the place I really want. My wants were usually based on the cuisine. And it was the same on the other side as well. So it was never the same or a close alternative to the other.

I dont't really consider a conflict here. It was more like there was no consideration of the other. Maybe that was the conflict. Anyways, we would end up with the choice thats not mine.

I would get angry but tried my best to enjoy the evening. I'm a foodie, so it was tough. But I couldn't find a good solution to this. So would end up agreeing, because it was more soothening than a ruined evening. So I thought this for a while. And all I came up with was to use my intuition for the days we might have the 'lets go out to eat' moment, and I would offer to pick up food.

It worked in a way, but if I had to go back and change, I would've discussed my feelings about the situation in the nicest way and hope for the best.
